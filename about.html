About

Jenny Core is a British Visual Artist living and working in Berlin, Germany, and exhibits her drawing works internationally. Core’s artist practice explores innovative ways of working in contemporary drawing.

Splats, lines and marks on a page, contribute to the creation of Core’s process led abstract drawings. On her investigation to experiment more with contemporary drawing, she draws with objects rather than observing objects. This process of chance and play, creates abstract scenes that are a vessel for many images.

Jenny Core’s artwork is featured in private and public collections across the globe, including UK, Berlin, Norway, Denmark, LA & NYC.
